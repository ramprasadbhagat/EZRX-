This Acceptable Use Policy (“AUP”) provides the rules and practices that platform participants (hereinafter referred to as “Users”) must comply with when accessing or using the eZRx+ online ordering platform and mobile application, and all their subsequent versions and iterations (“Platform”):

1. Users may only access and use the Platform according to the purpose for which the access is provided, namely to facilitate order submission on behalf of Zuellig Pharma’s authorised customers.

2. Users must not disclose or share their login credentials (namely username and password) with any person.

3. Users must not submit any orders on behalf of any customers without their explicit approval.

4. Users must ensure the details of any order they are submitting have been properly aligned with the relevant customer before submitting it for processing on the Platform so as to prevent ordering mistakes or duplications.

5. Users must not engage in any activity that violates any applicable laws, regulations or industry codes of conduct.

6. Users must not upload, send or post, enter or publish any information that is illegal, obscene, misleading, harassing, threatening, defamatory, inflammatory, indecent or otherwise injurious to third parties or objectionable; or in violation of any applicable law, regulations, industry code of conduct or a third party’s intellectual property and other proprietary or privacy rights.

7. Users must immediately report to the system administrator any violations or suspected violations of this policy or any compromise to or unauthorized use of their login credentials.

8. Users may only access, use and/or process any personal data accessed on the Platform solely for the purpose for which it was intended.

9. Users must not engage in spamming of any person or system.

10. Users must not transmit or input any data, send or upload any material that contains viruses, Trojan horses, worms, time-bombs, keystroke loggers, spyware, adware or any other harmful programmes or similar computer code designed to adversely affect the operation of any computer software or hardware, including but not limited to that of the Platform.

Zuellig Pharma has the right, but not the obligation, to monitor or investigate any User’s use of the Platform at any time for compliance with this AUP. Our determination of whether a violation of this AUP has occurred will be final and binding, and any action taken with respect to enforcing this AUP, including taking no action at all, will be at our sole discretion.

Zuellig Pharma may revise this AUP, including the list of restricted or prohibited activities, at any time by posting a revised version on the Platform. A User is deemed to have agreed to the revised version of the AUP if the User accesses or uses the Platform after the revised version of the AUP has been posted. 

Failure to adhere to this AUP may result in the suspension or termination of the User’s access and use of the Platform. The system administrator is entitled to share any User’s personal information and activities on the Platform with any law enforcement agency.

If you disagree with any part of this AUP, please discontinue your access and use of the Platform and notify us immediately to cancel your login credential.
